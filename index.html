Our Story

Growing Dreams, Nurturing Nature

Summerville Nursery has been dedicated to providing the finest quality plants and landscaping solutions for our valued customers. Our passion for horticulture and commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in the industry.

From humble beginnings, we have grown to operate two branches, each offering a wide variety of plants, from ground covers to majestic trees, potted plants to landscaping supplies.
