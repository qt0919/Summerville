Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Thriving Nursery

Summerville Nursery began with a simple passion for plants and a dream to share the beauty of nature with our community. What started as a small garden has grown into a trusted name in the horticultural industry.

Over the years, we have expanded from our original location to now operate two branches, each dedicated to providing the highest quality plants, expert advice, and exceptional customer service.

Our journey has been driven by our love for greenery and our commitment to helping our customers create beautiful, sustainable gardens and landscapes.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by our values, driven by passion

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the highest quality plants and landscaping solutions while promoting sustainable gardening practices and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

To be the leading nursery in the region, recognized for our exceptional plant quality, innovative landscaping designs, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, sustainability, and customer-first approach guide everything we do. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers.
