Professional Landscaping Services

At Summerville Nursery, we bring your outdoor vision to life. From residential gardens to commercial landscapes, our experienced team creates beautiful, sustainable spaces that enhance property value and provide years of enjoyment.

We provide landscaping services across Selangor and the Klang Valley, including Rimbayu, Tropicana Aman, Eco Sanctuary, Parkland, Jenjarom, Kota Kemuning, Bandar Botanic, Bandar Bukit Tinggi, Bukit Raja, Setia Alam, Klang, Shah Alam, Subang Jaya, Puchong, Petaling Jaya, Meru, Kapar, Port Klang, USJ, Cyberjaya, Putrajaya, Sepang, Banting, Dengkil, Kuala Langat, Kuala Selangor, Rawang, Bestari Jaya, and beyond.

Our Landscaping Process

How we bring your vision to life

1

Consultation

We meet with you to understand your vision, preferences, and budget for your outdoor space.

2

Site Analysis

Our team assesses your property's conditions including soil, drainage, sunlight, and existing features.

3

Design & Planning

We create a detailed landscape design with plant selections and layout for your approval.

4

Installation

Our experienced crew brings the design to life with quality plants and materials from our nursery.

5

Follow-up Care

We provide guidance on maintenance and offer ongoing care services if needed.
