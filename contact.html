Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open daily from 8:30 AM to 6:30 PM at both branches.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services for larger orders and landscaping materials. Contact us for delivery rates and availability.

Do you accept wholesale orders?

Absolutely! We welcome wholesale orders from landscapers, contractors, and businesses. Contact us for wholesale pricing.

Can I get landscaping consultation?

Yes, we offer professional landscaping consultation services. Contact us to schedule an appointment with our landscape team.

Which branch has more variety?

Do you provide plant care advice?

Yes! Our experienced staff is always happy to provide plant care tips and advice for your purchases.
